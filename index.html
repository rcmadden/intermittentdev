Book: A Smarter Way To Learn April 7, 2014

This book is unique in that the chapters are short readings (around 10 minutes or less for most) followed by an exercise and 20 online questions to reinforce learning in such a way that it sticks. I completed the 90 lessons in 5 weeks. The Kindle book table of contents outlined 88 chapters however, there were 90 chapters. It was quite disappointing when I got to the "end" to find I had 2 lessons left. I took another 10 days to actually build the sample site and get this site online. I built my version of the sample site below, writing all the HTML and CSS myself (no copy paste and no googling) as well as the first draft of this site, which still needs a lot of improvement. I'm very happy I spent the time to work through this book as Mark Myers delivers on the title.
