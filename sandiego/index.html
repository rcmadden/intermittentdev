One of the best climates in America.

San Diego is one of the top-ten best climates in the Farmers' Almanac and is one of the two best summer climates in America as scored by The Weather Channel.

Take a moment to explore San Diego and discover it's many attractions as well as the culture and the people that make San Diego great.

Check out San Diego's many beaches and tourist attractions such as Balboa Park, Belmont amusement park, San Diego Zoo, San Diego Safari Park, and SeaWorld. San Diego's Spanish and Mexican heritage is reflected in many historic sites across the city, such as Mission San Diego de Alcala and Old Town San Diego State Historic Park. Also, the local craft brewing industry attracts an increasing number of visitors for "beer tours" and the annual San Diego Beer Week in November; San Diego has been called "America's Craft Beer Capital" according to Wikipedia.

San Diego has 52 distinct neighborhoods and over 100 communities, home to many events and attractions.

You could spend a month exploring San Diego and never see the same thing twice.
